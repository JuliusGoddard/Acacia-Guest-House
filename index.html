An escape from the hustle and bustle

Nestled in the heart of South East England, Surrey is a county renowned for its lush countryside, charming villages, and rich heritage. Just a short journey from London, it offers the perfect balance between peaceful rural living and vibrant urban access, making it an ideal escape for visitors looking to unwind without straying too far from the capital.

Surrey is home to rolling hills, scenic woodlands, and picturesque riverside towns such as Guildford, Farnham, and Dorking. Nature lovers can explore the Surrey Hills Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, with its winding trails, panoramic views, and historic landmarks like Box Hill and Leith Hill Tower. For those interested in history and culture, the county boasts a wealth of stately homes, gardens, and museums that reflect its storied past.

Whether you're strolling through a local market, enjoying a traditional pub lunch, or discovering hidden gems along the River Wey, Surrey offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere. It’s a destination that invites visitors to slow down, relax, and take in the timeless charm of the English countryside.

Surrey also offers a vibrant cultural scene, with theatres, galleries, and seasonal festivals that showcase local talent and creativity. The Yvonne Arnaud Theatre in Guildford hosts a rich mix of performances, from classic plays to modern productions, while smaller towns feature artisan markets and music events that bring communities together throughout the year. For families, Surrey is packed with attractions that entertain and educate. From the thrills of Thorpe Park to the fascinating exhibits at Brooklands Museum and the beautifully landscaped RHS Garden Wisley, there’s something to capture every interest. Wildlife lovers can enjoy days out at Bocketts Farm Park or the British Wildlife Centre, where you can get up close to native animals in naturalistic settings.

Dining in Surrey is a treat in itself. With a wealth of countryside pubs, independent cafés, and fine dining restaurants, the local food scene blends traditional British fare with modern culinary flair. Many venues source their ingredients from nearby farms and producers, offering a true taste of the region. Whether you’re craving a cream tea in a cosy tearoom or a candlelit dinner overlooking the hills, Surrey delivers with charm and flavour.

Acacia Guest House is the perfect way to treat yourself and give you access to what Surrey has to offer.
